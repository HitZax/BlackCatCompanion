# 🐱 Black Cat Companion - Stress Relief App

A minimalist, interactive stress-relief web app featuring a cute black cat with glowing yellow eyes. Perfect for professionals who need a moment of calm during their workday.

## ✨ Features

### 🐾 **Pet & Pat Interactions**

- Click "Pet" to trigger a purring animation
- Click "Pat" to trigger a bouncing animation
- Each interaction triggers happy hearts and facial expressions
- The cat reacts with a cute smile and eye shine

### 🗑️ **Task Purge System**

- Type any stressor or task that's bothering you
- Click "Release" to watch your stress literally shred and disappear
- Sweet visual animation with satisfying shredding effect
- Clears the input automatically for the next release

### 💪 **Hyper-Focus Mode**

- Click and hold on the cat's eyes for 3 seconds
- Activates a 25-minute Pomodoro-style focus timer
- Dark blue focus mode background transition
- Track time remaining with real-time counter
- Perfect for deep work sessions

### ✨ **Motivators**

- Get inspirational quotes and affirmations
- Choose your motivator source:
  - **Hybrid**: Mix of curated quotes and live APIs (recommended)
  - **Curated**: Hand-picked, high-quality motivational quotes
  - **Live API**: Random affirmations from online APIs
- Fallback quotes if APIs are unavailable
- Each new motivator triggers happy animation

### 🌙 **Night Mode**

- Opens the app after 8:00 PM?
- The cat's eyes transform into sleepy slits
- Receives a gentle reminder: "Still working? The cat says it is time to log off"
- Encourages healthy work-life balance

### 🏆 **Logic Reward**

- Type "Done" or "Finish" into the task purge box
- Release it to unlock special celebration
- Cat performs a slow, satisfied blink
- Reward message: "Efficiency achieved. Rest now."
- Heart burst animation for extra cuteness

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Interact with the cat by petting or patting it
3. Use the "Release" button to purge stressful tasks
4. Hold on the cat's eyes for 3 seconds to start focus mode
5. Get motivational quotes with the "New motivator" button
6. Enjoy the calming presence of your black cat companion
